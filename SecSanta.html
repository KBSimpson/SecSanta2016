UMBC Crew Secret Santa 2016

Same rules as last year apply:

  • Gift must be under 20$

  • No YuGiOh cards. Paraphernalia (e.g.: mats, deckboxes, etc.) is fine.
  • If you want to give other gifts outside of the secret santa, feel free.
